# Quick Start Guide

You're 15-20 minutes away from having a video script writer that sounds like you on camera.

## Step 1: Open the Folder

Open Claude Code and point it at your `ai-shortform-kit` folder.

## Step 2: Follow the Setup

Claude will guide you through:

1. **Brand** — Tell Claude about your content and audience. Have a brand doc? Drop it in.
2. **Persona** — This is the big one. Best if you can share links or transcripts of 3-5 existing videos. No videos? Claude will interview you about your on-camera style. Or pick a creator to emulate.
3. **Creators** — Name the creators you admire. Claude will study what makes them effective.
4. **Config** — Which platforms, how often, what length.
5. **Trends** — Optional research on what's working in your niche right now.

## Step 3: Start Scripting

After setup, start a **new chat** (important — fresh context window).

Then just tell Claude what you want:
- "Write a script about productivity myths"
- "Give me 5 scripts for the week"
- "What's trending in my niche?"
- "Script a myth-bust about morning routines"

## Tips

- **Share your videos.** The more video samples you provide, the better Claude matches your on-camera persona.
- **Pick your hooks.** Claude generates 5 hook options for every script. The hook IS the video — choose carefully.
- **Use the filming guide.** Every script comes with shot lists, text overlay specs, and editing notes.
- **Research trends regularly.** Run `/research-trends` every week or two to keep your playbook fresh.
- **Batch your content.** Use `/batch-scripts` to plan and write a week of content in one session.
