# ai-shortform-kit

Your AI-powered short-form video script writer. Get scripts for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts that sound like you on camera — with hooks, timestamps, visual cues, and filming guides.

## What This Is

A Claude Code workspace that learns your brand and on-camera persona, then writes video scripts that sound like you'd say them naturally. Complete with timing, visual direction, text overlay cues, and a filming guide.

No APIs. No packages. No dependencies. Just you and Claude.

## What You Need

- [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code)
- That's it.

## What You Get

- **Your brand on file** — positioning, audience, content pillars, platform strategy
- **Your on-camera persona, captured** — an 8-dimension analysis of how you present on camera, from energy level to hook style to humor
- **6 skills** — scripting, batching, trend research, persona capture, brand setup, creator management
- **11 script templates** — hook-story-CTA, myth-bust, listicle, tutorial, hot take, storytime, and 5 more — each with timing, visual direction, and retention mechanics
- **A content playbook** — pre-loaded with platform algorithms, retention principles, and production conventions
- **An autonomous agent** — generates scripts from scratch based on your brand and persona

## Getting Started

Open a terminal, navigate to your `ai-shortform-kit` folder, and start Claude Code. It will walk you through setup.

### First Time Setup (~15-20 minutes)

1. Your brand profile
2. Your on-camera persona (bring video links/transcripts if you have them)
3. Your inspiration creators
4. Your content configuration (platforms, niche, frequency)
5. Optional: initial trend research in your niche

After setup, start a new chat and tell Claude what to script.

## Available Commands

| Command | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| `/script` | Write a single video script (concept, hooks, script, filming guide) |
| `/batch-scripts` | Generate a week of scripts at once |
| `/research-trends` | Research what's trending in your niche |
| `/digest-brand` | Create or update your brand profile |
| `/persona-capture` | Capture or refresh your on-camera persona (8 dimensions) |
| `/add-creator` | Manage your inspiration creators |

Just talk naturally — "write a script about morning routines" works perfectly.

## How Scripts Look

Every script includes:
- Timestamps for each section
- Visual direction (camera angles, B-roll cues)
- Text overlay specifications
- Tone and energy notes
- Spoken words in quotes
- A filming guide with shot list and editing notes

## FAQ

**Do I need existing videos?**
No, but they help. Claude can capture your persona through an interview or by studying creators you admire.

**What platforms does it support?**
TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Scripts can be adapted across platforms.

**How does it know my on-camera style?**
It analyzes 8 dimensions: energy, hooks, voice, humor, visual style, pacing, authority, and closing patterns.
